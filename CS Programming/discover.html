Discover UMD & Duluth

Where Lake Superior meets inspiration ✨

Why I Chose UMD

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because it’s the perfect blend of strong academics, a supportive community, and breathtaking surroundings. From the moment I visited, I felt connected to the peaceful energy of the campus and the kindness of the people here. UMD offers an environment where learning feels both challenging and comforting — a space that truly feels like home.

  • Beautiful Campus
  • Close Community
  • Strong Academics
  • Lakeside Serenity
  • Welcoming Atmosphere

What I Love About Duluth

Duluth is one of those rare places where nature, creativity, and warmth come together. I love the views of Lake Superior, the vibrant local coffee shops, and the friendly spirit that flows through the city. Whether it’s walking the Lakewalk or hiking at Enger Tower, Duluth reminds me to slow down and appreciate life’s beauty.

Campus Experiences

My journey at UMD has been filled with memorable moments — late-night study sessions at the Library, laughter in Kirby Student Center, and quiet reflection by the lake. These experiences have shaped who I am and made Duluth not just a college town, but a second home.
